3/31 (2 Hours):
Continuing Phase 3: Included Exclusive Attention Toggle (in config as well):
The Mathematical Formula:
    If y_i is the output of standard self-attention for position i, and v_i is the self-value vector, 
    the exclusive output z_i is defined by subtracting the projection of y_i onto v_i:
    z_i = y_i - proj(y onto v)
    By removing this component, z_i becomes mathematically orthogonal to v_i, ensuring the attention layer 
    only passes on information that the token didn't already "know" about itself.
 
Rethought the excessive normalization in the router:
    normalizing before the sigmoid selection reduces the magnitude / volume is BAD
    reason: sigmoid doesn't care about the other values when calculating it's sigmoid (softmax does (via the division of the summation))
    ngpt: Angles matter, volume (module / magnitude) is dangerous.
    router (outside): Volume = Confidence
    we are applying a sigmoid THRESHOLD, so like we need this to not be normalized
    removed all normalization in the router that doesn't deal with the hidden states directly 
    (so removed latent / classification score normalization)
